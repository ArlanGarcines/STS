Welcome to Salvador Trade School

Executive Summary

Salvador Trade School envisions becoming a leading Center of Technical Proficiency Polytechnic Institute by 2028. We are committed to providing inclusive, quality education that equips trainees with both technical skills and essential work values, empowering them for global competitiveness. Our mission is to produce skilled graduates prepared for today’s job market and long-term career success. We foster diversity and accessibility, ensuring all learners have the opportunity to thrive. To achieve our goals, we will implement an Institutional Development Plan that aligns our curriculum with industry needs. Through strong partnerships with industry, we’ll keep our programs relevant and impactful. At the core of our vision is the desire to empower individuals to build sustainable livelihoods and contribute meaningfully to their communities. More than education, it’s about opportunity, growth, and lasting impact.
